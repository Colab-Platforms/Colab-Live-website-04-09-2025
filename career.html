Digital Marketing Executive (1+ Years)

Location: Goregaon East, Mumbai

Job Description:

Are you a digital-first thinker who thrives on scaling brands through performance marketing? We're looking for a Digital Marketing Manager to lead our online growth across paid media, quick-commerce platforms, and e-commerce marketplaces.

What You will do:

  • Strategize & execute high-performing campaigns across all paid channels – Meta, Google, YouTube, Programmatic, Affiliates, etc.
  • Optimize ROAS through data-driven decisions and real-time performance tracking.
  • Drive visibility, traffic, and conversions on Quick-Commerce platforms (e.g., Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart) and E-commerce Marketplaces (e.g., Amazon, Flipkart).
  • Analyze competitor strategies, market trends, and audience insights to stay ahead of the game.

  • Collaborate with creative, product, and sales teams to ensure cohesive digital narratives and promotions.

Requirements:

  • Prior experience in FMCG, D2C, Q-Commerce, or E-commerce domains is highly preferred
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
