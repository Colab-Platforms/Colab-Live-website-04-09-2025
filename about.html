OUR IP'S

Seven Ways to Power Your Game One Place for Every Service.

Contact Us
1. Sports Technology

The Sports Ecosystem Marketplace is a one-stop platform where athletes and sports lovers can Learn, Play, and Shop all in one place. Book top coaches and academies to level up your game, play by finding and booking turfs, stadiums, and other sports infrastructure nearby, and shop for high-quality gear, accessories, and nutrition from trusted brands. Its your all-in-one hub to train, compete, and gear up with ease.

2. Sports IP

Colab is actively developing and managing impactful sports IPs that span multiple formats and audiences, combining competitive excellence with strong market appeal. Our flagship IPs include Northern Challengers in the Big Cricket League, Australian Champions in the World League of Legends, and Punjab De Sher in the Celebrity Cricket League. Each IP is designed with a unique identity, catering to distinct fanbases and market segments.

3. Sports Content

Colab delivers dynamic and engaging sports content that keeps fans, athletes, and organizations connected and informed. From live match updates, highlights, and behind-the-scenes stories to athlete features and expert insights, we create immersive experiences that bring the sports world to life. Our platform offers high-quality sports news, in-depth analysis, blogs, and multimedia content along with match insights, expert commentary, and performance analytics.

4. Athlete management

At Colab, our athlete management program is designed to nurture emerging sports stars while supporting established talent. We focus on identifying and developing promising athletes by providing them with expert guidance, performance tracking, and opportunities to compete at higher levels. From training and mentorship to branding and career planning, we offer end-to-end support empowering rising athletes to showcase their potential and helping professionals manage their growth, visibility, and success on and off the field.

5. Sports Marketing

Sports marketing at Colab goes beyond promotion it's about building meaningful connections between athletes, brands, and fans. We leverage data-driven strategies, influencer engagement, and immersive digital campaigns to amplify visibility, drive fan loyalty, and unlock new revenue streams. Whether it's Field events or global leagues, Colab creates powerful narratives that elevate the reach and impact of every sport.

6. Sports Infrastructure

At Colab, we don't just build facilities, we create ecosystems where athletes, communities, and sports thrive. From high-performance training centers to multi-sport arenas, our infrastructure solutions are designed to inspire excellence and foster engagement.
