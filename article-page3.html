COLAB PLATFORMS TO INCORPORATE “COLAB INTELLIGENCE PVT. LTD.”

Strengthening Presence in USD 200 Billion Artificial Intelligence Sector

Colab Platforms Limited (“Colab” or “the Company”), a diversified technology company, has announced plans to incorporate Colab Intelligence Pvt. Ltd., a wholly owned subsidiary. This strategic move marks a significant milestone in Colab’s long-term vision to strengthen its presence in the rapidly expanding Artificial Intelligence (AI) sector, valued at approximately USD 200 billion globally in 2024 and projected to exceed USD 1.8 trillion by 2030, growing at a CAGR of over 30%.

The initiative reflects Colab Platforms’ commitment to innovation, digital transformation, and the integration of advanced AI-driven technologies across its business ecosystem. Colab Intelligence will focus on developing cutting-edge solutions such as intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and collaborative AI platforms that enhance decision-making, operational efficiency, and human–machine synergy.

This move strategically positions Colab Platforms to capture high-growth opportunities across multiple transformative industries. The expansion will diversify its technology portfolio, unlock new revenue streams, and drive sustainable, long-term value for shareholders.

AI Opportunities Across Sectors

  • Global AI market projected to exceed USD 1.8 trillion by 2030.
  • Gaming & Esports AI market to surpass USD 583 billion by 2030.
  • Sports analytics and AI-driven management forecast to reach USD 19 billion by 2030.

Key Growth Drivers

  • Intelligent automation & predictive analytics.
  • Collaborative AI platforms enhancing decision-making.
  • AI integration across gaming, fintech, and retail for scalable innovation.

AI Impact Across Industries

Gaming & Esports

Hyper-personalization, immersive gameplay, and predictive analytics for players and organizers.

Sports Analytics

AI-enhanced performance tracking and fan engagement for professional sports.

Financial & Retail

AI applications projected to surpass USD 100 billion by 2030 with intelligent automation and customer insights.

Leadership Perspective

“This marks a new chapter in the company’s growth journey, aimed at boosting innovation and market relevance. Colab Intelligence is central to building a resilient, diversified business model that captures value from emerging technologies. By focusing on AI-driven industries, Colab Platforms aims to unlock new revenue streams and strengthen its position as a forward-looking leader in India’s tech landscape.”

Mr. Puneet Singh
Managing Director, Colab Platforms Limited

About Colab Platforms

Colab Platforms Limited is a diversified technology, sports and digital ventures company focused on building integrated ecosystems spanning esports, physical sports infrastructure, sporting events, influencer collaborations, and digital content delivery. With a vision to strengthen India’s sports and youth economy, Colab is creating sustainable value across multiple verticals.
